AISpace2

A Jupyter extension for learning and understanding AI.

What?

AISpace2 exists in two parts: as an extension for Jupyter Notebook, a web application that allows you to work with rich documents that combine code, text, and visualizations; and as a series of notebooks that teach and demonstrate AI concepts by integrating Python code implementations with interactive visualizations that are powered by the extension.

AISpace2 is the next version of AISpace, and accompanies the book Artifical Intelligence 2E: Foundations of Computational Agents by David Poole and Alan Mackworth.

How It Works

By combining Python code, explanatory text, and interactive visualizations, Jupyter notebooks and AISpace2 makes learning AI fun and easy.
